# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We expect all contributors to keep collaboration technical, respectful, and focused on the project goals.

## Expected behavior

- Use clear and professional language.
- Give constructive, evidence-based feedback.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Keep discussions on-topic and actionable.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith reviews.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict repository participation for behavior that violates this policy.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately via GitHub security/advisory channels or by contacting the maintainer account.
